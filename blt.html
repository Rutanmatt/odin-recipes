BLT

Description

The Absolute Best BLT

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bacon
  • Green Leaf Lettuce
  • Tomato

Instructions

  1. Prepare Bacon: Cook your bacon according to package instructions. I like to save on time and use pre-cooked bacon and warm it in my air fryer.
  2. Cut and Season Veggies: Slice the tomato and wash the lettuce. If you want to enhance the flavor of the tomato, season it with salt and pepper. Herbs are great too! A sprinkle of Italian seasoning adds a great pop of flavor.
  3. Assemble: To assemble the sandwich, toast the bread and spread the mayonnaise on one side of each slice. I usually toast my bread in the toaster, but for a more gourmet sandwich, you can toast it in a skillet with a little bit of butter.
  4. Add Fixings: Layer the bacon, lettuce, and tomato on one slice of bread. Top with the other slice of bread and enjoy!
